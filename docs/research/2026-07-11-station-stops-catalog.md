## Inter-station distances (approx. km)

**Updated:** 2026-07-19  
**Purpose:** Drive distance-proportional progress-rail segment widths in the game (`segmentKm` in `web/src/data/stations.ts`).  
**Accuracy:** Visual feel, not survey-grade. Prefer Wikipedia / KAI Commuter distance tables; fill gaps with OSM-adjacent estimates and fare-table totals. Round to ~0.1 km.  
**Start node:** The UI keeps a synthetic Start; Start → first station = mean of that line’s `segmentKm` (computed at runtime, not stored below).  
**List order:** Distances follow **consecutive game-list entries**, not a full network graph. Discontinuities are flagged.
